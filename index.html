Hello Reader I'm Sdrow Vieli

Writing novels and short stories is my passion. I like to think that when you write what you feel, readers feel what you write. I know, it sounds like a platitude, but honestly it simply sums up what I've set out to achieve by creating this site; a place where I can write what I feel and in doing so hope my hypothesis is correct.
Stories; whether fact or fiction are a glimpse into someones mind, every turn of a page takes you on a journey of discovery. You get to not only witness, but live through someone elses life, and through this link see the delicate, yet complicated dance between logic, emotions and situations on which our decisions are made, decisions that show people who we are and shape who we become.
​ The stories you'll read here aren't about myself or anyone I know, but somehow just like any other story they seem to encompass both, because no person can create an authentic character without injecting a piece of themselves, or someone they know into them. So sit back, relax and meet my characters; I hope you find a glimpse of yourself in them. - Sdrow Vieli ©
