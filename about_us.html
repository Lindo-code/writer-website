SHORT STORIES

Defined in the dictionary as works of fiction that are shorter than a novel. I've always been fascinated by fiction, especially by how much of it can be seen as fact and how much as fabrication, you always walk a thin line between the two when attempting to write good fiction. It's like trying to be a good liar; tell too much facts and risk revealing the entire truth, yet if you add too much fabrication you risk making your lie too obvious, the key is in finding an equlibrium between the two. So below I make an effort at weaving good lies, and I start small. - Sdrow Vieli Â©
